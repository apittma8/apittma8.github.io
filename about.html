About Me (Bio) - Alexander Pittman

 

My path in information technology and college-level learning began early: in the summer of 2020, right after sixth grade, I enrolled in a college Introduction to Computer Programming course, where the work came naturally and I discovered a strong interest in writing code and seeing it evolve into a finished product. By the spring of 2022, I transitioned to full-time college enrollment and completed the remainder of my high school graduation requirements there. In the fall of 2024, at age sixteen, I earned my Associate of Science with distinction, and I currently maintain a 3.95 GPA in my combined undergraduate/graduate program.

Outside of developing skills through my IT couresework, I enjoy working on personal coding projects, game development, and reading. I am also currently employed as a Student Technician at the Center for Architecture and Environmental Design, where I support their computer and printing lab.
