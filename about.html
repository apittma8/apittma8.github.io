About Me (Bio) - Alexander Pittman

 

My path in information technology and college-level learning began early. In the summer of 2020, right after sixth grade, I enrolled in a college Introduction to Computer Programming course, where the work came naturally and I discovered a strong interest in writing code and seeing it evolve into a finished product. By the spring of 2022, I transitioned to full-time college enrollment and completed the remainder of my high school graduation requirements there. At age sixteen, I earned my Associate of Science (with distinction). Most recently, during this last spring 2026 and two days after turning eighteen, I obtained my Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (summa cum laude).

Outside of developing skills through my coursework, I enjoy working on personal coding projects, game development, and reading. Currently, I am employed by Ohio CAT / Ohio Machinery Co. as a Systems Intern. During academic semesters, I work as a Student Technician at the Center for Architecture and Environmental Design, where I support their computer and printing lab.
